About me

  • I'm a former English teacher turned web developer.
  • Working mostly in Drupal, I like building stuff that everyone can use.
  • I'm interested in the interface between technology and humanity. And whether it should exist at all.
  • If you want to get in touch, reach out via Linkedin , Drupal Slack, or Mastodon.

About this blog

  • This site was inspired by a blog post about the 'rule of least power'.
  • It uses a modified version of bashblog to keep things as simple as possible.
  • There are only a few lines of JavaScript on this website. They are used to remember your [light / dark] mode selections.
